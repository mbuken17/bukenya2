Next Stop, PhD:
I will join Duke University (School of Medicine) to pursue a PhD in Immunology starting this Fall.
Over the past 3 years, I have developed keen interest in understanding various health conditions - infectious diseases, cancers and neurodegenerative diseases- and how these interact with the immune system. I have learned that this undestanding is crucial to identifying druggable targets and hence to developing therapies, vaccines and prophylactics. Thus, it is logical that I should seek to continue to explore this exciting field of possibilities! I believe that Duke's school of medicine is one of the best places to carry out this endeavor.
I got into many outstanding PhD programs and this is thanks to strong backing from more than just a few people. I'm thankful to my MIT PI (Darrell), my Tufts PI (Hyunmin) and my former manager at Biogen (Jenny) for backing me unreservedly through their letters of recommendations; My friends Fernando Senjobe (PhD student at Harvard) and Ivan Pires (PhD student at MIT) for providing a tonne of advice on my application materials; My host family (the Loughlins and the Mahoneys) for cheerleading me throughout the application process; My friends and colleagues with whom I shared the struggles of the application process - Ina (who got into Johns Hopkins), Shuhao (got into EPFL, Switzerland) and Josetta (Upenn).
